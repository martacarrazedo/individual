- ABOUT ME -

My name is Marta Carrazedo and I was born in Oporto, Portugal. I am graduating in Communication Design at FBAUP.


I am currently in the third year of college, I like especially graphic design , branding and illustration. I'm also part of a junior company, Olimpia Junior Consulting at ISCAP, which I belong to the communication department playing the designer role.
For me Design was the best way I found to express myself, an extension of my communication skills, an expression of something that words cannot express. I want to make beautiful things, somehow make a difference in the world.
Design won't save the world but it damn sure makes it look good.
